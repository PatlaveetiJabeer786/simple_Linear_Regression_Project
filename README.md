## 🔴 Business Problem  

Companies often need to **predict continuous outcomes** such as sales, revenue, or demand.  
Without predictive modeling:  
- ❌ Decisions rely on intuition instead of data  
- ❌ No framework to estimate future values  
- ❌ Business growth opportunities are missed  

➡️ The goal is to apply **Linear Regression** to predict numeric outcomes and demonstrate its use in real‑world forecasting.

| ⚙️ **Model Building** | Train Linear Regression model |
| 📈 **Evaluation** | Assess R², MSE, RMSE |
